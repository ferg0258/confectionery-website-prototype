About

Coco's Chocolatiers was founded in 1889 by Grandmother Coco. Over the years, Coco's family has perfected the secret family recipe including their famous chocolate in pastries, drinks, ice cream, and even on its own. Come see for yourself at Coco's Chocolatiers!
